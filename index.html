Game Overview

Find the Vampire is an exciting online mystery game that challenges your deduction skills. Set in a mysterious village, your mission is to identify the vampire who is hiding among the innocent villagers before it's too late.

How to Play

Observe the behavior of each character carefully. The vampire will exhibit subtle differences compared to normal villagers. Use your detective skills to analyze movements, reactions, and patterns to identify the vampire before they claim more victims.
